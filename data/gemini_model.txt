Gemini is a family of multimodal large language models developed by Google DeepMind, serving as the successor to LaMDA and PaLM 2. Gemini is designed to be natively multimodal, meaning it can understand, operate across, and combine different types of information including text, images, video, audio, and code. It is available in various sizes such as Ultra, Pro, and Flash, each optimized for different use cases.
